Here is how to make a mango milkshake

Ingredients

  • Mangoes
  • Milk
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Put mango in the blender
  2. Put milk in the blender
  3. blend
  4. Put honey in the blender
  5. blend
  6. Pour the mix into a container of your choice

There you have it. Enjoy
